DESCRIPTION
This project was built to spread knowledge about a digital artistic movement. The thought behind it is to spread a little-known artistic movement such ASCII art with the most popular magazine in Italy: Settimana Enigmistica. This is built as a special edition of the magazine focused entirely on ASCII Art, every content of every page is designed with text, and every element is about history or fun fact of ASCII Art. This is the combination of two opposite worlds, a digital one, and a tangible, that unite with the purpose of creating a unique and fascinating medium.
