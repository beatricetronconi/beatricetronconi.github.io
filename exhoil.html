DESCRIPTION
Exhoil is a speculative project, the aim of it is to make humans reflect on the consequences of their actions on nature, in this case, on soil exploitation. This brand is based on a scenario built in 2050, when humans will need to start to breathe for the soil to re-establish the carbon cycle, in this way, mankind will be submissive to nature. Exhoil was born as an oil company, exploiting resources from the soil, but in 2050, due to the carbon crisis, the company decided to invest in restoring carbon in the soil. The petrol station now have breathing columns in which human beings have to breathe everyday. Humans are now a resource for the natural world. Mankind is paying for its action, and this is the price. This is the only chance to survive and eat the products born from the soil.
