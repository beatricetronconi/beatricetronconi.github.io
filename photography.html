DESCRIPTION
Since 2021, I have cultivated a strong interest in analogue photography, dedicating much of my free time to exploring this medium with my Olympus mju II and Olympus am100 cameras.What I find most fascinating about analogue photography, especially as someone with a perfectionist approach, is the unpredictability of the process: the anticipation and delayed gratification of waiting to see the developed images, and the unique imperfections that give each photo its own character.
