DESCRIPTION
Trulli Alberobello is a group project designed to promote the UNESCO World Heritage site of the Trulli di Alberobello in a way that avoids traditional tourist clich√©s and commercial approaches. We developed a visual identity that respects the unique spirit of the site and explored creative strategies to communicate both the history and the symbolism of these iconic constructions. The project includes a range of artifacts inspired by the Trulli: institutional materials, playing cards, two booklets, a wayfinding system, posters, and branded gadgets such as t-shirts, hats, and stickers. We also designed a mobile application that gathers essential information and interesting facts about Alberobello, helping visitors discover hidden stories and lesser-known aspects of the Trulli. The goal is to guide and inspire tourists and locals to experience Alberobello in a more authentic and meaningful way.
